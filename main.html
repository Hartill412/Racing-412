About Me

In my family there's just me and my parents. I started riding at 2yrs old on a Yamaha TTR50. Then I got older and taller and got a KX65 for Christmas in 2012. I raced my KX65 for the first time in June 2015 and took 2nd overall in the 65cc Beginner class. I had seven more races in 2016 on my 65 including one 1st place overall in The Dirt Series. Then outgrew my 65 and got a KX85 which I raced five times with The Dirt Series and SDMX Summer Nights Racing. In 2017 I've raced my 85 six times including my first Transworld Event where I placed 3rd overall in the 85cc Intro Beginner Class. I began training in 2017 with BJ Burns from MX University to become a much better rider.

Riding Goals

I ride because riding means so much to me! My main goal is to be the 85cc Beginner and 85cc Open overall points leader in The Dirt Series at the end of the season. My second goal is to qualify for Mammoth at Pala Raceway later this year. My final goal is to keep training with BJ Burns from MX University and hopefully make it to a Loretta Lynn's Qualifier down the road.

Competitive Highlights

2015: First ever race, 65cc Beginner, won 2nd place overall.

2016: 7 races on KX65 including my first 1st place overall in 65cc Open. 5 races on KX85 including a 1st place overall with SDMX Summer Nights Racing in the 85cc 1st Timers Class.

2017: Battling at the top of the points in The Dirt Series for the 85cc Beginner and 85cc Open Classes. First time taking 1st place overall in 2 classes at the same event, The Dirt Series Rd1 at Glen Helen Raceway. Took 3rd place overall in my class the first time I raced a Transworld Event.

2020: I took 1st place at SoCal OTMX at Perris Raceway.
